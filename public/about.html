About Me

Software tinkerer, Retro technology enthusiast, Strange sounds advocate.

In my "spare" time, I host the radio program 56 kbit/s on WRIR 97.3FM where I explore the intersection of technology and sound.

I am also co-creator and maintainer of the lazy sundays blog. A site dedicated to exploring "games, music, film, art, and technology."

I graduated from Virginia Tech in 2023 with my degree in Computer Science and I currently work as a Software Engineer in Richmond, VA. However, I hope to transition to a more creative and expressive position in the near future.
